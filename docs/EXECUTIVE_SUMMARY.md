# 📊 ExamCraft AI - Executive Summary

> **AI-Powered Exam Question Generation Platform**  
> **Transforming Education Through Intelligent Assessment**

---

## 🎯 Executive Overview

**ExamCraft AI** is a production-ready SaaS platform that leverages Claude AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to automate the creation of high-quality exam questions for OpenBook assessments. We help educators save 80% of their time while improving question quality and pedagogical alignment.

### Key Value Proposition

| Traditional Method | ExamCraft AI | Improvement |
|-------------------|--------------|-------------|
| 6 hours per exam | 45 minutes | **87% faster** |
| Manual quality checks | AI-powered validation | **25% higher quality** |
| Limited reusability | Centralized question bank | **Unlimited reuse** |
| No analytics | Data-driven insights | **Continuous improvement** |
