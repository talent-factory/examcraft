# Quick Start

Create your first AI exam in 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Modern browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Internet connection
- ExamCraft AI account

## Step 1: Sign In

1. Open ExamCraft AI in your browser
2. Sign in with your Google account or email
3. After signing in, you will see the dashboard

## Step 2: Upload Document

1. Click **Upload Documents** in the navigation
2. Drag a PDF or Word document into the upload area (or click **Select Files**)
3. Wait until processing is complete (10–45 seconds)

!!! tip "Tip"
    Structured documents with clear headings produce better exam questions.

## Step 3: Create Exam

1. Switch to the **Create RAG Exam** tab
2. Select your uploaded documents
3. Configure:
    - **Topic/Focus**: e.g., "Sorting Algorithms"
    - **Number of Questions**: 5–10 recommended
    - **Question Types**: Multiple Choice and/or Open-ended
    - **Difficulty Level**: Easy / Medium / Hard
4. Click **Generate RAG Exam**

## Step 4: Review Results

After 20–60 seconds, you will see your generated questions with:

- Question text and answer options
- Correct answer with explanation
- Source references from your documents
- Confidence score — quality indicator
